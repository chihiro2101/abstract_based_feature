mary of burgundy was born in brussels at the ducal castle of coudenberg , to charles the bold , then known as the count of charolais , and his wife isabella of bourbon her father controlled a vast and wealthy domain made up of the duchy of burgundy , the free county of burgundy , and the majority of the low countries nicholas i , duke of lorraine , was a few years older than mary and controlled a duchy that lay alongside burgundian territory , but his plan to combine his domain with hers was ended by his death in battle in 1473 mary assumed the rule of her father 's domains upon his defeat in battle and death on 5 january 1477 mary soon made her choice among the many suitors for her hand by selecting archduke maximilian of austria , the future holy roman emperor maximilian i , who became her co-ruler mary loved riding and was hunting with maximilian and knights of the court when her horse tripped , threw her in a ditch , and then landed on top of her , breaking her back mary 's marriage into the house of habsburg proved to be a disaster for france because the burgundian inheritance later brought it into conflict with spain and the holy roman empire
