mary of burgundy was born in brussels at the ducal castle of coudenberg , to charles the bold , then known as the count of charolais , and his wife isabella of bourbon later she was approached by charles , duke of berry ; his older brother , king louis xi of france , was intensely annoyed by charles 's move and attempted to prevent the necessary papal dispensation for consanguinity nicholas i , duke of lorraine , was a few years older than mary and controlled a duchy that lay alongside burgundian territory , but his plan to combine his domain with hers was ended by his death in battle in 1473 mary assumed the rule of her father 's domains upon his defeat in battle and death on 5 january 1477 the king was anxious that mary should marry his son charles and thus secure the inheritance of the low countries for his heirs , by force of arms if necessary burgundy , fearing french military power , sent an embassy to france to negotiate a marriage between mary and the six-year-old dauphin ( later king charles viii ) , but returned home without a betrothal ; the french king 's demands of cession of territories to the french crown were deemed unacceptable mary 's marriage into the house of habsburg proved to be a disaster for france because the burgundian inheritance later brought it into conflict with spain and the holy roman empire
