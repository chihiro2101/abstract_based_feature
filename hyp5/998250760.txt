the first nazi party rallies took place in 1923 in munich and in 1926 in weimar each rally was given a programmatic title , which related to recent national events : 1923 : the first party congress took place in munich on january 27 , 1923 1927 : the 3rd party congress ( '' day of awakening '' ) was held in nuremberg , august 19–21 , 1927 1929 : the 4th party congress , known as the '' day of composure '' , was held in nuremberg , august 1–4 , 1929 1933 : the 5th party congress was held in nuremberg , august 30 – september 3 , 1933 the leni riefenstahl film der sieg des glaubens was made at this rally spiegel.de ( german ) 1934 : the 6th party congress was held in nuremberg , september 5–10 , 1934 , which was attended by about 700,000 nazi party supporters propaganda in nazi germany 1935 : the 7th party congress was held in nuremberg , september 10–16 , 1935 1936 : the 8th party congress was known as the '' rally of honour '' ( reichsparteitag der ehre , september 8–14 ) it celebrated the reduction of unemployment in germany since the nazi rise to power official films for the rallies began in 1927 , with the establishment of the nsdap ( nazi party ) film office
