the old town , noto antica , lies directly north on mount alveria a city of sicel origin , it was known as netum in ancient times according to legend , daedalus stayed in the city after his flight over the ionian sea , as did hercules after his seventh task in 866 it was conquered by the muslims , who elevated the city to become a capital of one of the three valli of sicily ( the val di noto ) in 1091 , it became the last islamic stronghold in sicily to fall to the christians in 1503 king ferdinand iii granted it the title of civitas ingeniosa ( '' ingenious city '' ) in the following centuries , the city expanded , growing beyond its medieval limits , and new buildings , churches and convents were built the medieval town of noto was virtually razed by the 1693 sicily earthquake storia della città di noto , by salvatore russo ferruggia , pappalardo publisher , 1838 ) : page 66 it was decided to re-build the town at the present site , on the left bank of the river asinaro , closer to the ionian shore noto cathedral ( cattedrale di san nicolò di mira , finished in 1776 ) church of the collegio di san carlo church of the sacro nome di gesu santissimo salvatore : church and benedictine convent church of san nicola di mira noto vlbi home page sicilian baroque val di noto roman catholic diocese of noto noto in sicily webcam on cathedral of san nicolò il portale su noto accommodation center of noto
