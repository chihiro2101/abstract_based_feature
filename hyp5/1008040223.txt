the first polish tourists were pilgrims traveling to shrines both within poland and abroad the most popular regions were mountains , especially the tatra mountains , explored for example by tytus chałubiński after poland regained independence in 1918 , polish tourism boomed , and was encouraged by the government after world war ii all tourist organizations were nationalized by the new communism government this was the era of governmentally-founded tourism , characterised by mass but low-standard tourism a typical sight was a holiday campground with small bungalows managed by one of the state-owned companies poland has a diversified natural environment , which is relatively unaffected by human development jasna góra monastery was visited by 3.6 & nbsp ; million of pilgrims from 78 countries in 2014
