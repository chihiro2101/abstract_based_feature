to prevent this , in october 1184 william released his aunt constance from monastery , approved her marriage and designated her as his heir in june 1185 , tancred led a huge sicilian fleet of 300 ships under the command of richard , count of acerra his brother-in-law to durazzo to attack the core of the byzantine empire the sicilian army was then heavily damaged by the army of the byzantine emperor isaac ii and was annihilated on the way back to the balkans , while the fleet of tancred returned to sicily unharmed despite having sworn fealty to constance , as soon as william ii died , in 1189 , tancred rebelled and seized control of the island roger of andria , also a candidate for sicilian throne , was among the supporters of constance and henry matthew d'ajello managed to defame roger , and in the same year richard , count of acerra brother-in-law of tancred tricked roger into captivity and execution richard immediately demanded the release of his sister , william ii 's wife joan , imprisoned by tancred in 1189 , along with every penny of her dowry and dower ( in response of her vocal support of germans ) richard was joined in sicily by the french crusading army , led by king philip ii richard and philip recognised tancred as king of sicily and vowed to keep the peace among all three of their kingdoms despite this tancred always treated his aunt , now detained , honourably with courtesy , which his wife queen sibylla strongly opposed , believing this would implicitly acknowledge the claim of the latter but his death at palermo ( 20 february 1194 ) two months after that of his young son and co-king , roger iii , opened the way for hohenstaufen rule in sicily tancred 's children with sibylla were : roger iii , duke of apulia and king of sicily william iii , duke of apulia and king of sicily elvira , countess of lecce after the death of her brother ;
