ezra vogel was born to joseph and edith vogel , a family of jewish immigrants in 1930 in delaware , ohio while attending ohio wesleyan , vogel was a member of the beta sigma tau fraternity ( later merged with the pi lambda phi fraternity ) after discharge , vogel enrolled in the department of social relations at harvard and graduated with his phd in 1958 after two years of field work in japan , he worked as an assistant professor at yale university from 1960–1961 , but returned to harvard for post-doctoral work on chinese language and history he was director of harvard 's east asian research center from 1972–1977 and chairman of the council for east asian studies from 1977–1980 he also was director of the program on us–japan relations at the center for international affairs from 1980–1987 , and was named honorary director upon stepping down vogel published dozens of articles , reviews , conference papers , and books on china , japan , and american–east asian relations , and organized scholarly and policy conferences vogel 's later research areas included industrialization , changes in family structure , political change , and security issues in south korea , hong kong , taiwan and east asia overall he continued publishing after his retirement : his last two books were deng xiaoping and the transformation of china ( 2011 ) and china and japan : facing history ( 2019 ) one of the resulting conference volumes was china at war : regions of china , 1937–1945 ( 2007 ) , co-edited with stephen r mackinnon , diana lary deng xiaoping and the transformation of china ( 2011 ) the park chung hee era : the transformation of south korea ( 2011 ) china and japan : facing history ( 2019 ) '' tributes to professor ezra vogel ( 1930 – 2020 ) , '' ( 13 january 2020 ) harvard university asia center .
