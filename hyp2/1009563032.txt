meltham also includes the small village of helme which has its own school and church surrounding villages within the kirklees area include crosland moor , golcar , honley , holmfirth , linthwaite , marsden , netherton and slaithwaite meltham itself contains three primary schools , namely meltham church of england ( c of e ) school , meltham moor primary school and helme ( c of e ) junior and infant school secondary schools serving meltham primarily include honley high school , holmfirth high school and colne valley high school , all of which are located in neighbouring areas meltham is home to the meltham and meltham mills band , which was established in 1846 as a brass band meltham mills was the former site of jonas brook and brothers , a silk mill complex that employed over 1,000 workers during the late 19th century the brook family were philanthropists and built housing in meltham mills for their employees , including the convalescent home they also built meltham hall , helme church and owned an estate at enderby in leicestershire prior to the boundary change in 1896 , parts of meltham mills were within the township of honley boundary image : meltham_village viewed from wessenden moor.jpg view of meltham from wessenden moor , huddersfield is in the far distance image : david brown tractors , meltham mills ( rlh ) jpg david brown tractor factory meltham , 1981 image : david_brown_990_implematic_tractor.png david brown 990 implematic tractor made in meltham around 1964 image : meltham cub scouts , late 1970s ( rlh ) jpg meltham cub scouts , on blackpool bridge , near digley reservoir ( late 1970s ) image : royd edge.jpg meltham viewed from royd edge
