john hume was born in 1937 in derry , the son of anne ( née doherty ) and samuel hume hume was a student at st columb 's college and at st patrick 's college , maynooth , the leading catholic seminary in ireland and a recognised college of the national university of ireland , where he intended to study for the priesthood he was a founding member of the credit union movement in the city and was chair of the university for derry committee in 1965 hume was prominent in the unsuccessful fight to have northern ireland 's second university established in derry in the mid-sixties after this campaign , john hume went on to be a prominent figure in the derry citizens ' action committee hume , a founder member of derry credit union , became the youngest ever president of the irish league of credit unions at age 27 he once said that '' all the things i 've been doing , it 's the thing i 'm proudest of because no movement has done more good for the people of ireland , north and south , than the credit union movement hume became an independent nationalist member of the parliament of northern ireland in 1969 at the height of the civil rights campaign in 1977 , hume challenged a regulation under the civil authorities ( special powers ) act ( northern ireland ) 1922 which allowed any soldier to disperse an assembly of three or more people hume held the tip o'neill chair in peace studies at the university of ulster , currently funded by the ireland funds hume held the position of club president of his local football team , derry city f.c on his death , former labour leader and prime minister tony blair said : '' john hume was a political titan ;
