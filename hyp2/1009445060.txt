oshawa is the site of lakeridge health oshawa , formerly oshawa general hospital lakeridge health oshawa is also home to lakeridge health education and research network ( lhearn ) that opened in 2013 oshawa is also home to the oshawa clinic , the largest , multi-specialty medical group practice in canada policing in oshawa is provided by the durham regional police service there are two police stations in oshawa one at 77 centre street north in the downtown area , and a south oshawa community policing centre on cedar street oshawa fire services operated by the city operates from six fire stations located throughout the city public education in oshawa is provided by the durham district school board oshawa is a community teaching site for queen 's university school of medicine at lakeridge health the main campus of durham college is located in the city in 2010 , the university opened a stand-alone campus in oshawa ( beside the oshawa civic recreation complex ) both stations are owned by durham radio , which also owns cjkx , which is licensed to the nearby community of ajax , although all three stations are operated from the same studios at the oshawa executive airport
