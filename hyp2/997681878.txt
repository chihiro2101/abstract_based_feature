scaliger was born at agen , the tenth child and third son of italian scholar julius caesar scaliger and andiette de roques lobejac an outbreak of the plague in 1555 caused the boys to return home , and for the next few years joseph was his father 's constant companion and amanuensis the composition of latin verse was the chief amusement of his father julius in his later years , and he would daily dictate to his son between eighty and a hundred lines , and sometimes even more joseph was also required each day to write a latin theme or declamation , though in other respects he seems to have been left to his own devices he learned from his father to be not only a scholar , but also an acute observer , aiming at historical criticism more than at correcting texts after his father 's death he spent four years at the university of paris , where he began the study of greek under adrianus turnebus it was to dorat that scaliger owed his home for the next thirty years of his life , for in 1563 the professor recommended him to louis de chastaigner , the young lord of la roche-posay , as a companion in his travels here they found muretus , who , when at bordeaux and toulouse , had been a great favourite and occasional visitor of julius caesar scaliger at agen muret soon recognized the young scaliger 's merits , and introduced him to many contacts well worth knowing scaliger 's preface to his first book , the conjectanea in varronem , is dated there in december 1564 on his return to france he spent three years with the chastaigners , accompanying them to their different châteaux in poitou , as the calls of the civil war required the massacre of st bartholomew — occurring as he was about to accompany the jean de montluc on an embassy to poland — made scaliger flee , together with other huguenots , for geneva , where he was appointed a professor in the academy he hated lecturing , and was bored with the importunities of the fanatical preachers ; and in 1574 he returned to france and made his home for the next twenty years with chastaigner of his life during this period we have interesting details and notices in the lettres françaises inédites de joseph scaliger , edited by tamizey de larroque ( agen , 1881 ) his commentary on manilius is really a treatise on ancient astronomy , and it forms an introduction to de emendatione temporum ; in this work scaliger investigates ancient systems of determining epochs , calendars and computations of time in the remaining twenty-four years of his life he expanded on his work in the de emendatione he declined ; he hated lecturing , and there were those among his friends who erroneously believed that with the success of henry iv learning would flourish , and protestantism would be no barrier to his advancement midway through 1593 he set out for the netherlands , where he would pass the remaining sixteen years of his life , never returning to france at the same time , scaliger had made numerous enemies nor does scaliger attempt a refutation of the crucial point , namely , that william , the last prince of verona , had no son nicholas , who would have been the alleged grandfather of julius pattison had made many manuscript collections for a life of joseph scaliger on a much more extensive scale , which he left unfinished the fragments of the life of joseph scaliger have been printed in the essays , i grafton , joseph scaliger : a study in the history of classical scholarship , 2 vol correspondents of scaliger : joseph justus scaliger maintained a vast correspondence with european humanists and scholars , whose names are listed here the correspondence of joseph justus scaliger in emlo
