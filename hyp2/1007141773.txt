lansbury 's term as leader was dominated by foreign affairs and disarmament , and by policy disagreements within the labour movement the official party position was based on collective security through the league of nations and on multilateral disarmament lansbury , supported by many in the plp , adopted a position of christian pacifism , unilateral disarmament and the dismantling of the british empire 107–08 under his influence the party 's 1933 conference passed resolutions calling for the '' total disarmament of all nations '' , and pledged to take no part in war 109–10 pacifism became temporarily popular in the country ;
