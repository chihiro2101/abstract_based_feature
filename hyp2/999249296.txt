newton was born in berlin , the son of klara '' claire '' ( née marquis ) and max neustädter , a button factory owner newton attended the heinrich von treitschke-realgymnasium and the american school in berlin interested in photography from the age of 12 when he purchased his first camera , he worked for the german photographer yva ( elsie neuländer simon ) from 1936 he was issued with a passport just after turning 18 and left germany on 5 december 1938 after arriving in singapore he found he was able to remain there , first briefly as a photographer for the straits times and then as a portrait photographer newton was interned by british authorities while in singapore and was sent to australia on board the queen mary , arriving in sydney on 27 september 1940 in 1948 , he married actress june browne , who performed under the stage name june brunell helmut newton 's australian years - guy featherstone the studio was renamed 'helmut newton and henry talbot ' newton and wife finally settled in paris in 1961 and the work continued as a fashion photographer playboy : helmut newton , chronicle books ( 2005 ) klaus honnef & helmut newton , helmut newton : portraits , schirmer art books , 1986 helmut newton , playboy : helmut newton , chronicle books , 2005
