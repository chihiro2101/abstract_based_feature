newton was born in berlin , the son of klara '' claire '' ( née marquis ) and max neustädter , a button factory owner interested in photography from the age of 12 when he purchased his first camera , he worked for the german photographer yva ( elsie neuländer simon ) from 1936 after arriving in singapore he found he was able to remain there , first briefly as a photographer for the straits times and then as a portrait photographer newton was interned by british authorities while in singapore and was sent to australia on board the queen mary , arriving in sydney on 27 september 1940 the exhibition of 'new visions in photography ' was displayed at the federal hotel in collins street and was probably the first glimpse of new objectivity photography in australia newton and wife finally settled in paris in 1961 and the work continued as a fashion photographer a heart attack in 1970 reduced newton 's output , nevertheless his wife 's encouragement led to his profile continuing to expand , especially with a big success , the 1980 studio-bound stark infinity of the '' big nudes '' series helmut newton : the photography icon newton shot a number of pictorials for playboy , including pictorials of nastassja kinski and kristine debell all three became friends with helmut and june newton and to varying degrees assisted helmut newton ( this is one of his numerous editorials in french vogue , that 's not a book ) helmut newton , world without men , new york : xavier moreau , 1984 klaus honnef & helmut newton , helmut newton : portraits , schirmer art books , 1986 helmut newton , playboy : helmut newton , chronicle books , 2005
