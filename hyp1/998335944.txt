these rocks are said to have been thrown down into morioka by an eruption of mt when the people prayed to the spirits of mitsuishi for protection , the devil was immediately shackled to these rocks and forced to make a promise never to trouble the people again as a seal of his oath , the devil made a handprint on one of the rocks , thus giving rise to the name iwate , its direct translation
