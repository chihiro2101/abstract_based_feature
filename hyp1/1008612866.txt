the olympic bidding process begins with the submission of a city 's application to the international olympic committee ( ioc ) by its national olympic committee ( noc ) and ends with the election of the host city by the members of the ioc during an ordinary session during the first phase , which begins immediately after the bid submission deadline , the '' applicant cities '' are required to answer a questionnaire covering themes of importance to a successful games organisation these files are carefully studied by the ioc evaluation commission , a group composed of ioc members , representatives of international sport federations , nocs , athletes , the international paralympic committee , and international experts in various fields the evaluation commission communicates the results of its inspections in a report sent to the ioc members up to one month before the electing ioc session the ioc session in which a host city is elected takes place in a country that did not submit an application to stage the olympics the election is made by the assembled active ioc members ( excluding honorary and honour members ) , each possessing one vote following the announcement of the host city , the successful bid delegation signs the '' host city contract '' with the ioc , which delegates the responsibilities of the games organisation to the city and respective noc if a bid 's score was higher than six ( ioc 's predefined benchmark score ) , the city was considered highly capable of hosting the games ;
