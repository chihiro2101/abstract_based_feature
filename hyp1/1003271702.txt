clarithromycin is primarily used to treat a number of bacterial infections including pneumonia , helicobacter pylori , and as an alternative to penicillin in strep throat other uses include cat scratch disease and other infections due to bartonella , cryptosporidiosis , as a second line agent in lyme disease and toxoplasmosis it is effective against upper and lower respiratory tract infections , skin and soft tissue infections and helicobacter pylori infections associated with duodenal ulcers spectrum of bacterial susceptibility hypersensitivity to clarithromycin or any component of the product , erythromycin , or any macrolide antibiotics qt prolongation or ventricular cardiac arrhythmias , including torsade de pointes the most common side effects are gastrointestinal : diarrhea ( 3 % ) , nausea ( 3 % ) , abdominal pain ( 3 % ) , and vomiting ( 6 % ) it also can cause headaches , insomnia , and abnormal liver function tests common adverse effects of clarithromycin in the central nervous system include dizziness , headaches clarithromycin should not be used in pregnant women except in situations where no alternative therapy is appropriate for lactating mothers it is not known whether clarithromycin is excreted in human milk unlike erythromycin , clarithromycin is acid-stable , so can be taken orally without having to be protected from gastric acids clarithromycin is available as a generic medication
