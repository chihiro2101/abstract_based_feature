image : jerash bw 12.jpg thumb 300px the oval forum and cardo of gerasa ( jerash ) the names of the traditional ten cities of the decapolis come from natural history ( pliny ) except for damascus , the decapolis cities were by and large founded during the hellenistic period , between the death of alexander the great in 323 bc and the roman conquest of coele-syria , including judea in 63 bc some of the cities included '' antiochia '' or '' seleucia '' in their official names ( antiochia hippos , for example ) , which attest to seleucid origins the decapolis was a region where two cultures interacted : the culture of the greek colonists and the indigenous semitic culture there is evidence that the colonists adopted the worship of other semitic gods , including phoenician deities and the chief nabatean god , dushara ( worshipped under his hellenized name , dusares ) the people of the hellenized cities welcomed pompey as a liberator from the jewish hasmonean kingdom that had ruled much of the area when pompey reorganized the region , he awarded a group of these cities with autonomy under roman protection thumb under roman rule , the cities of the decapolis were not included in the territory of the herodian kingdom , its successor states of the herodian tetrarchy , or the judea ( roman province ) instead , the cities were allowed considerable political autonomy under roman protection the imperial cult , the worship of the roman emperor , was a very common practice throughout the decapolis and was one of the features that linked the different cities the decapolis was one of the few regions where jesus travelled in which gentiles were in the majority : most of jesus ' ministry focused on teaching to jews most of the cities continued into the late roman and byzantine periods
