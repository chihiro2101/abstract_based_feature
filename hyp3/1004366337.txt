image : jerash bw 12.jpg thumb 300px the oval forum and cardo of gerasa ( jerash ) the names of the traditional ten cities of the decapolis come from natural history ( pliny ) , in ellicott 's commentary for english readers on matthew 4 , accessed 6 december 2016 according to other sources , there may have been as many as eighteen or nineteen greco-roman cities counted as part of the decapolis except for damascus , the decapolis cities were by and large founded during the hellenistic period , between the death of alexander the great in 323 bc and the roman conquest of coele-syria , including judea in 63 bc others were founded later , when the seleucid dynasty ruled the region the decapolis was a region where two cultures interacted : the culture of the greek colonists and the indigenous semitic culture there is evidence that the colonists adopted the worship of other semitic gods , including phoenician deities and the chief nabatean god , dushara ( worshipped under his hellenized name , dusares ) the people of the hellenized cities welcomed pompey as a liberator from the jewish hasmonean kingdom that had ruled much of the area when pompey reorganized the region , he awarded a group of these cities with autonomy under roman protection thumb under roman rule , the cities of the decapolis were not included in the territory of the herodian kingdom , its successor states of the herodian tetrarchy , or the judea ( roman province ) the imperial cult , the worship of the roman emperor , was a very common practice throughout the decapolis and was one of the features that linked the different cities the decapolis came under direct roman rule in ad 106 , when arabia petraea was annexed during the reign of the emperor trajan jerash ( gerasa ) and bet she'an ( scythopolis ) survive as towns today , after periods of abandonement or serious decline
