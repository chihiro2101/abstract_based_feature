the son of a roman eques of the name of capito , musonius rufus was born in volsinii , etruria suda μ 1305 , musonius about 20–30 ad by the time of nero , he was already famous in rome , where he taught stoic philosophy he was associated with the stoic opposition against the perceived tyranny of nero he followed rubellius plautus into exile when plautus was banished by nero ( 60 ad ) musonius was able to survive and form a small community of philosophers 81 when the party of vitellius gained the upper hand , musonius was able to accuse , and obtain the conviction of , publius egnatius celer , the stoic philosopher who had condemned barea soranus 10 , 40 it was perhaps about this time that musonius taught epictetus , his most famous student so highly was musonius esteemed in rome that vespasian allowed him to remain in rome when the other philosophers were banished from the city ( 71 ad ) , cassius dio , lxv 13 but eventually he was exiled anyway ( perhaps around 75 ad ) , only returning after vespasian 's death ( 79 ad ) his philosophy consists entirely of the rules for the conduct of life ; all knowledge ought to be serviceable to action his precepts for the simple life are carefully detailed , and he gives precise regulations for diet , the care of the body , clothing , and even furniture dillon , ( 2004 ) , musonius rufus and education in the good life : a model of teaching and living virtue rufus musonius musonius rufus entry in the internet encyclopedia of philosophy online edition of lectures and fragments
