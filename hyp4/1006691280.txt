in 843 , the treaty of verdun placed dendermonde in lotharingia otto ii built a fort here in the 10th century , encouraging further settlements in the area the town received its city charter in 1233 and grew quickly after that thanks to a thriving cloth industry a cloth hall and belfry were erected on the market square in the mid 14th century the 16th century saw a decline in dendermonde 's fortunes the city was then fortified by the austrians against further french ambitions on august 19 , 2006 , 28 prisoners managed to escape dendermonde prison the city hall and belfry have also been designated a world heritage site since 1999 notable people from dendermonde
