cecil calvert was born on 8 august 1605 in kent , england , to george calvert , who was later created , in 1625 , the 1st baron baltimore , and anne mynne ( or mayne ) calvert entered trinity college at the university of oxford in 1621 his father , the 1st baron baltimore , converted to catholicism in 1625 , and it is likely that his children followed him ; at least his sons did in 1628 , cecil accompanied his father , lord baltimore , along with most of his siblings and his stepmother , to the new colony of newfoundland cecil calvert succeeded as the 2nd baron baltimore upon his father 's death in april 1632 . settlement of the maryland colony lord baltimore , as cecil now was , received a charter from king charles i for the new colony of maryland , to be named for the queen consort , henrietta maria ( wife of king charles i ) this was shortly after the death , in april 1632 , of his father , the 1st baron baltimore , who had long sought the charter to found a colony in the mid-atlantic area to serve as a refuge for english roman catholics this charter would be heavily contested by the 2nd lord baltimore 's heirs and the penn family in the penn–calvert boundary dispute
