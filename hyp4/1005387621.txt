descendant of the alepoudelis , an old olive oil industrial family from lesbos , elytis was born in heraklion on the island of crete , on 2 november 1911 his family later moved to athens , where the poet graduated from high school and later attended courses as an auditor at the law school at university of athens odysseus elytis – biographical nobelprize.org in 1935 elytis published his first poem in the journal new letters ( νέα γράμματα ) at the prompting of such friends as george seferis his entry with a distinctively earthy and original form assisted to inaugurate a new era in greek poetry and its subsequent reform after the second world war during the war he was appointed second lieutenant , placed initially at the 1st army corps headquarters , then transferred to the 24th regiment , on the first-line of the battlefields elytis was sporadically publishing poetry and essays after his initial foray into the literary world in 1960 he was awarded the first state poetry prize , in 1965 the order of the phoenix and in 1975 he was awarded the doctor honoris causa in the faculty of philosophy at thessaloniki university and received the honorary citizenship of the town of mytilene elytis and teriade had formed a strong friendship that solidified in 1939 with the publication of elytis first book of poetry entitled '' orientations '' in 1948 he was the representative of greece at the international meetings of geneva , in 1949 at the founding congress of the international art critics union in paris and in 1962 at the incontro romano della cultura in rome elytis ' theoretical and philosophical ideas have been expressed in a series of essays under the title the open papers ( ανοιχτά χαρτιά ) the room with the pictures ( το δωμάτιο με τις εικόνες , 1986 ) – collages by
