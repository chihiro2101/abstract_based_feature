under the direction of the ministry of health and family welfare ( mohfw ) , india 's national human rights commission drafted a charter of patients ' rights in 2018 . recognized patients ' rights the charter of patients ' rights lists seventeen rights that patients are entitled to : right to information : every patient has the right to know what is the illness that they are suffering , its causes , the status of the diagnosis ( provisional or confirmed ) , expected costs of treatment right to emergency care : public and private hospitals have an obligation to provide emergency medical care regardless of the patients ' capacity to pay for the services physicians should clearly explain the risks from receiving the treatment and only administer the treatment after getting explicit written consent from the patient right to confidentiality , human dignity and privacy : doctors should observe strict confidentiality of a patient 's condition , with the only exception of potential threats to public health right to transparency in rates , and care according to prescribed rates wherever relevant : hospitals should display the rates that they charge in a visible manner and patients should receive an itemized bill when payment is required right to patient education : in addition to information about their condition , patients have the right to know about public health services such as insurance schemes and charitable hospitals in the united states there have been a number of attempts to enshrine a patient 's bill of rights in law , including a bill rejected by congress in 2001 although both bills would have provided patients key rights , such as prompt access to emergency care and medical specialists , only the senate-passed measure would provide patients with adequate means to enforce their rights consumer patient rights and responsibilities advisory commission on consumer protection and quality in the health care industry american hospital association -
