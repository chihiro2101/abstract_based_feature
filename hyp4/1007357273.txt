traditional outfit himba people , especially women , are famous for covering themselves with otjize paste , a cosmetic mixture of butterfat and ochre pigment otjize cleanses the skin over long periods due to water scarcity and protects from the hot and dry climate of the kaokoland , as well as from insect bites it gives himba people 's skin and hair braids a distinctive texture , style , and orange
