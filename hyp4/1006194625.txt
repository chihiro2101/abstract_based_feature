the president of israel is elected by an absolute majority in the knesset , by secret ballot . from 1949 to 2000 , the president was elected for a five-year term , and was allowed to serve up to two terms in office if the president is temporarily incapacitated , or leaves office , the speaker of the knesset becomes acting president the first presidential election took place on 16 february 1949 , and the winner was chaim weizmann the second took place in 1951 , as at the time presidential terms were linked to the length of the knesset term ( the first knesset lasted only two years ) unlike most presidents in parliamentary republics , the president of israel is not even the nominal chief executive israel 's electoral system and fractured political landscape make it all but impossible for one party to govern alone , let alone win an outright majority of knesset seats the president also serves as the main speaker at the opening ceremonies of the half-yearly knesset conference , as well as at the annual official ceremonies for yom hazikaron and yom hashoah gov.il : president of the state of israel official website knesset.gov.il : president of the state of israel official website
