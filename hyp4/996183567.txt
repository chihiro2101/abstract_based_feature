lambton was born 12 april 1792 in the house of his father william henry lambton at 14 berkeley square in st george hanover square ( now within the city of westminster ) lambton was christened with the names of his grandfathers , john lambton and george villiers in 1825 lambton took the chairmanship of the new zealand company , a venture that made the first attempt to colonise new zealand , with his interest being philanthropic durham was first elected to parliament for county durham in the general election of 1812 , a seat he held until 1828 , when he was raised to the peerage as baron durham , of the city of durham and of lambton castle in the county palatine of durham in parliament he supported liberal causes , from the defence of queen caroline to the removal of political disabilities on dissenters and roman catholics lord durham resigned from cabinet in 1833 later the same year he was further honoured when he was made viscount lambton and earl of durham in 1838 , lord durham was named governor general and high commissioner for british north america a biography of john george lambton , first earl of durham ( 1929 ) p 375 one of his tasks as governor general was to investigate the circumstances surrounding the lower canada rebellion of louis-joseph papineau and the upper canada rebellion of william lyon mackenzie , which had occurred in 1837 lord durham is less well regarded for recommending the union of upper and lower canada , which resulted in the creation of the united province of canada lord durham 's mission to canada : a biography of john george lambton , first earl of durham clarendon press , 1929 ) online life and letters of the first earl of durham : 1792â€“1840 ( 2 vol london : longmans , green and company '' 'radical jack ' : john george lambton , first earl of durham
