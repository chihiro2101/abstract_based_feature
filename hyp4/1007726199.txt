reforms of ratichius and with the report of these reforms issued by the universities of jena and giessen in 1627 he led the brethren into exile when the habsburg counter-reformation persecuted the somewhat visionary projects in 1639 , comenius published his pansophiæ prodromus , and in the following year his english friend hartlib published , without his consent , the plan of the pansophic work
