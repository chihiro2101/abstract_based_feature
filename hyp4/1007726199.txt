john amos comenius was born in 1592 in the margraviate of moravia in the bohemian crown john was the youngest child and only son of martin komenský ( died 1602–4 ) and his wife anna chmelová 66 martin and anna komenský belonged to the moravian brethren , a pre-reformation protestant denomination , and comenius later became one of its leaders his parents and two of his four sisters died in 1604 and young john went to live with his aunt in strážnice owing to his impoverished circumstances he was unable to begin his formal education until later in life comenius was greatly influenced by the irish jesuit william bathe as well as his teachers johann piscator , heinrich gutberleth , and particularly heinrich alsted comenius became rector of a school in přerov he produced the book janua linguarum reserata , or the gate of languages unlocked , which brought him to prominence comenius took refuge in leszno in poland , where he led the gymnasium and , furthermore , was given charge of the bohemian and moravian churches in 1639 , comenius published his pansophiæ prodromus , and in the following year his english friend hartlib published , without his consent , the plan of the pansophic work as outlined by comenius the pansophic ideas find partial expression in the series of textbooks he produced from time to time comenius also attempted to design a language in which false statements were inexpressible in 1641 , he responded to a request by the long parliament and joined a commission there charged with the reform of the system of public education daniel murphy , comenius : a 606 texts and 406 tunes in other writings , comenius addresses both instrumental and vocal music in many places , although he dedicated no treatise to the topic musical practice , both instrumental and vocal , played an important role in his system of education the most permanent influence exerted by comenius was in practical educational work he was first a teacher and an organizer of schools , not only among his own people , but later in sweden , and to a slight extent in holland the influence of comenius on educational thought is comparable with that of his contemporaries , bacon and descartes , on science and philosophy the summary of this attempt is given in the didactica magna , completed about 1631 , though not published until several years later it was also the first
