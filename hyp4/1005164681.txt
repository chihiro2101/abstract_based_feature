in the 1160s , henry ii began to alter his policy of indirect rule in brittany and to exert more direct control local breton nobles rebelled against conan , so conan sought henry ii 's help in 1164 , henry intervened to seize lands along the border of brittany and normandy and , in 1166 , he invaded brittany to punish the local barons henry then forced conan to abdicate as duke and to give brittany to his five-year-old daughter , constance , who was handed over and betrothed to henry 's son geoffrey growing tensions between henry and louis vii of france finally spilled over into open war in 1167 , triggered by a trivial argument over how money destined for the crusader states of the levant should be collected henry was then free to move against the rebel barons in brittany , where feelings about his seizure of the duchy were still running high there is evidence to suggest that geoffrey was planning another rebellion with philip 's help during his final period in paris in the summer of 1186 as a participant in so many rebellions against his father , geoffrey acquired a reputation for treachery in the second version , in the chronicle of the french royal clerk rigord , geoffrey died of sudden acute chest pain , which reportedly struck immediately after his speech to philip , boasting his intention to lay normandy to waste after geoffrey 's death , henry ii arranged for constance , geoffrey 's widow , to marry ranulf de blondeville , 6th earl of chester ranulf would become duke of brittany , jure uxoris , for a short time before this marriage was annulled
